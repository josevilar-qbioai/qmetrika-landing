QMetrika philosophy

Capital Q — governed compute becomes capital

A token spent and forgotten is expense. A token spent, governed, and captured is capital. Three architectural decisions, not aspirations.

local.py

Data stays local

Genomic and sensitive data is processed on local models (PyTorch, ESMC). It never leaves your infrastructure. Required for SaMD classification and GDPR/HIPAA compliance.

gate.py

Fail-closed governance

The pipeline halts until a human authorizes. Approval broker, declarative RBAC policies, and a verifiable hash-chain audit. Control lives in the tool.

ip.md

Proprietary knowledge

Patented ΔG engine (P202630522, OEPM), international PCT filing April 2027. Every project leaves behind models, data, and evaluations that belong to you.
